Copper

Symbol: Cu
Atomic Number: 29
Atomic Mass: 63.55u
Colour: reddish brown
Odour: odourless
State (STP): Solid
Category: Metal
Melting Point: 1084°C
Boiling Point: 2562°C
Density: 8.9g/cm3
Fun Fact: The name copper comes from the island of Cyprus, where it was extensively mined in ancient times.
