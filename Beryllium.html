Beryllium

Symbol: Be
Atomic Number: 4
Atomic Mass: 9.012182 u
Colour: Steel-gray
Odour: Odourless
State (STP): Solid
Category: Metal
Melting Point: 1287 °C
Boiling Point: 2468 °C
Density: 1.848 g/cm³
Fun Fact: Beryllium is used in fusion power generators, as it is highly electron repellent. In addition, it is extremely toxic, so DO NOT eat it if you find it.
