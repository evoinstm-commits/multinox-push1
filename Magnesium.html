Magnesium

Symbol: Mg
Atomic Number: 12
Atomic Mass: 24.305 u
Colour: Silvery-white
Odour: Odourless
State (STP): Solid
Category: Metal
Melting Point: 650°C
Boiling Point: 1091°C
Density: 1.738 g/cm³.
Fun Fact: Magnesium is the 8th most abundant element in the earth's crust, making up 13 per cent of our planet's mass
