To begin, let's establish a foundational understanding of key chemical concepts.

What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the scientific discipline concerned with the composition, properties, and reactions of matter. It explores the transformations substances undergo and the formation of new materials.

What is an atom made up of?

An atom is composed of three primary subatomic particles: electrons, protons, and neutrons.

What is the Atomic Number?

The atomic number is defined by the unique count of protons found within the nucleus of an atom. This number is the fundamental identifier of a chemical element.

What is the Atomic Mass?

Atomic mass represents the total mass of an atom. It is primarily the sum of the masses of its protons and neutrons.

What is STP?

STP is an acronym for Standard Temperature and Pressure, a standardized set of conditions used for experimental measurements in chemistry and other sciences.
