Phosphorus

Symbol: P
Atomic Number: 15
Atomic Mass: 28.085 u
Colour: Dark grey with a bluish tinge
Odour: Garlic-like or match-like odor
State (STP): Solid
Category: Non-metal
Melting Point: 44.1°C
Boiling Point: 280°C.
Density: 1.82 g/cm3
Fun Fact: There are about 750 grams of phosphorus in the average adult.
